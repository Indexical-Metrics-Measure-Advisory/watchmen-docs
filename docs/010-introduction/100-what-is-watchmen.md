### Watchmen: The AI-Ready Data Platform
Watchmen is a next-generation **AI-ready**, **no-code** data platform built on distributed stream processing technology.

It provides comprehensive services covering data ingestion, transformation, governance, asset management, and insight generation, enabling organizations and ecosystem partners to unlock the full business value of their data.

### Why Watchmen?
As organizations accelerate their digital transformation, data has become a core production factor driving competitiveness, risk control, and business innovation. However, many teams face persistent challenges:
- Fragmented data across legacy systems
- Inconsistent reports and duplicated metrics
- Slow data delivery to business teams
- High cost of integrating new AI or analytics solutions

Watchmen addresses these challenges by providing a unified, no-code data platform that connects core business systems, eliminates data silos, and builds AI-ready data foundations for faster innovation.

### Our Vision: AI-Ready Data for Everyone
Watchmen helps teams move beyond simply "storing data" to understanding, reasoning, and acting on data. Through unified data models, reusable features, and intelligent metrics, teams can enable AI to:
- Understand the meaning and relationships of data
- Invoke and analyze data through APIs or natural language (NL2Insight)
- Explain insights and decisions with context and traceability
